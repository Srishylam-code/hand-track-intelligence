Architecture

Proprietary Technologies

Our system bridges the physical-digital divide by integrating state-of-the-art computer vision models with physics-based rendering engines, delivering an uncompromising volumetric experience.

Neural Pipeline Inference

Utilizing Google's MediaPipe, our high-performance machine learning node computes 21 spatial constraints per hand at true 60+ FPS, adapting dynamically to diverse lighting and occlusion scenarios.

Explore ML on GFG

Dynamic Topology Engine

By evaluating the continuous Euclidean distance between critical joint landmarks (e.g. thumb to index tip), the engine fluidly maps microscopic finger flexes directly to UI elements in 3D space.

Explore Math on GFG

Zero-Latency OSC Telemetry

To ensure the visualizer reacts concurrently to physical movement, massive multidimensional coordinate matrices are multiplexed via UDP into TouchDesigner over the Open Sound Control protocol.
